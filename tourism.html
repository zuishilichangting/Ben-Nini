合作流程Collaboration Process

  • 城市提案City Proposal
    双方确认合作城市、限定元素（地标/文化符号/配色）和目标产品线。Both parties confirm city, exclusive elements (landmarks/cultural symbols/palette), and target product line.
  • 角色设计Character Design
    在角色红线范围内设计城市专属配件（围巾/帽子/包包/吊牌），不改变角色本体。Design city-exclusive accessories within red-line constraints — swap accessories, never alter the character.
  • 打样确认Sampling & Approval
    按打板流程完成首样，双方确认外观、材质、包装和限定吊牌设计。Complete first samples per production specs, both parties approve appearance, materials, packaging, and exclusive tags.
  • 生产交付Production & Delivery
    按约定数量和交期完成生产，提供完整的包装、质检和销售资料。Complete production per agreed quantity and timeline with complete packaging, QC, and sales documentation.
  • 联合推广Joint Promotion
    双方在社交媒体联合发布：品牌方提供内容素材和文案，合作方提供线下陈列和渠道推广。Joint social media launch: brand provides content assets and copy, partner provides offline display and channel promotion.
