品牌故事Our Story

从一个想法，到一只只看得见的毛绒玩具。From an idea to plush toys you can hold.

2024

元年 · 菜菜街诞生Year One · Caicai Street is Born

东平湖边的风很暖。Ben 决定，要把他和 Nini 的故事，做成一只只看得见的毛绒玩具。他说："以前是你带我看世界，现在我想带着你的名字，去更远的地方。"The wind by Dongping Lake was warm. Ben decided to turn his story with Nini into plush toys you could hold. "You showed me the world," he said. "Now I want to take your name to even farther places."

2025

成型 · 三角色确立Forming · Three Characters Take Shape

Ben（可靠）、Nini（温柔）、Caibao（快乐）——三个角色的性格、轮廓、关系逐渐清晰。不是魔法世界，不是拯救故事，就是三个住在菜菜街的年轻人，认真生活，认真快乐。Ben (reliable), Nini (gentle), Caibao (joyful) — their personalities, silhouettes, and relationships took shape. No magic, no heroism. Just three young souls on Caicai Street, living sincerely and joyfully.

2026

出发 · 首批打样启动Launch · First Sampling Begins

完成品牌视觉体系、角色标准设定、毛绒打板资料包和工厂询价流程，正式走向从创意到产品的第一步。目标：让 Ben、Nini 和 Caibao 成为每个人身边触手可及的温暖同伴。Brand visuals, character standards, plush documentation, and factory RFQ process are complete. Step one: from creative vision to tangible product. The goal: make Ben, Nini, and Caibao warm companions you can reach out and touch.

菜菜街的世界观The World of Caicai Street

没有魔法，没有反派，只有一个很小很小的城市——菜菜街。这里每天发生的事都很普通：起床、吃饭、上班、发呆、旅游、吵架、和好。但每件事都特别真实。No magic. No villains. Just a tiny little place called Caicai Street. Every day is ordinary: waking, eating, working, daydreaming, arguing, making up. But it's all wonderfully real.
