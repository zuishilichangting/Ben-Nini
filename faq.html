品牌BrandBen & Nini 是什么？What is Ben & Nini?+
Ben & Nini（妮本）是一个关于陪伴、成长与日常快乐的原创角色品牌。三个角色——Ben（菜团/可靠小狗）、Nini（菜饼/温柔猫咪）和 Caibao（菜包/快乐包子）——住在菜菜街，过着普通但真实的生活。品牌核心价值：可靠、温柔、快乐。Ben & Nini is an original character brand about companionship, growth, and everyday joy. Three characters — Ben (reliable puppy), Nini (gentle cat), and Caibao (joyful bun) — live on Caicai Street, leading ordinary but wonderfully real lives. Core values: Reliable, Gentle, Joyful.
品牌Brand为什么叫"妮本"？Why "Nini & Ben"?+
英文品牌名 Nini & Ben 取自两位主角的名字。中文译名"妮本"——"妮"取 Nini 的"小妮儿"，"本"取 Ben 的"本哥"，合起来朗朗上口，适合中文市场传播。The English name "Nini & Ben" comes from the two main characters. The Chinese name "妮本" combines Nini's nickname "小妮儿" and Ben's nickname "本哥", creating a catchy name for the Chinese market.
品牌BrandBen & Nini 和其他 IP 有什么不同？What makes Ben & Nini different?+
不是魔法世界，不是战斗冒险，不是单纯情侣。Ben & Nini 是陪伴系生活 IP——三个角色代表了年轻人最向往的三种状态：可靠行动、温柔感受、快乐发现。用户不是"在看别人的故事"，而是"觉得自己就是Ben/Nini/Caibao"。No magic, no battles, not just a couple. Ben & Nini is a companion lifestyle IP — three characters representing what young people aspire to: reliable action, gentle feeling, and joyful discovery. Users don't "watch their story" — they feel like they ARE Ben, Nini, or Caibao.

产品Product毛绒玩具用什么材质？安全吗？What materials? Are they safe?+
外层使用超柔短毛绒（约280-330gsm），填充优质PP棉。所有小配件（眼镜、小花、蝴蝶结、星星）优先使用软布贴绣或绒布填充方案，避免硬质塑料件。首批产品按3+儿童玩具标准倒推设计，最终适用年龄以检测结果和渠道要求为准。Outer: ultra-soft short plush (~280-330gsm). Filling: premium PP cotton. All small accessories (glasses, flower, bow, star) use soft applique or plush-filled solutions. First batch designed with 3+ children's toy standards in mind; final age rating depends on testing and channel requirements.

工厂Factory打样需要多长时间？报价包含哪些？How long is sampling? What's included?+
首样制作一般7-15天，加上纸样/绣花测试3-7天，全程约2-4周。报价请明确：样品费、版费、MOQ、500/1000/3000只阶梯单价、打样周期、大货周期。同时说明是否含税/运费、贸易条款（EXW/FOB/DDP）、打样修改是否另收费、检测包装是否另计。详见打板文档第19章报价表。First sample: 7-15 days + pattern/embroidery testing 3-7 days, total ~2-4 weeks. Quote should specify: sample fee, mold/pattern fee, MOQ, tiered pricing (500/1000/3000), sample lead time, mass production lead time. Also clarify: tax/shipping inclusion, trade terms (EXW/FOB/DDP), revision fees, testing/packaging costs. See Chapter 19 of the production document.
工厂Factory工厂需要什么资质？What qualifications are needed?+
优先考虑：①有毛绒玩具出口经验，熟悉EN71/ASTM F963/CPC/REACH检测流程；②能提供材料清单并配合第三方检测；③有电脑刺绣和软质配件制作经验；④有类似IP毛绒案例。不具备出口经验的工厂也可先接国内订单。Preferred: ① Plush export experience, familiar with EN71/ASTM F963/CPC/REACH testing; ② Can provide material documentation and cooperate with third-party testing; ③ Machine embroidery and soft accessory experience; ④ Similar IP plush case studies. Factories without export experience can start with domestic orders.

跨境Intl海外版和国内版有区别吗？Any difference between international and domestic versions?+
产品本体统一，区别主要在包装和标签。海外版需要多语言吊牌/洗标（英文为主，预留日文韩文位）、目标市场合规标识（CE/UKCA/CPC等）、条码位和进口商信息位。角色外观和品质标准全球统一。Products are identical. Differences are in packaging and labeling. International versions require multilingual hang tags (primarily English, with space for Japanese/Korean), target market compliance marks (CE/UKCA/CPC), barcode placement, and importer info. Character appearance and quality standards are globally consistent.
