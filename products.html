材质与规格标准Material & Specification Standards

以下为毛绒玩具核心品类的材质标准，适用于所有 S 级产品。A/B/C 级产品按品类另行制定。Material standards for plush toys (S-Tier). Other tiers defined per category.

🧵
主体面料Outer Fabric

超柔短毛绒 / 水晶超柔 / 弹力短毛绒，约280-330 gsm，亲肤柔软，不可掉毛浮毛刺鼻气味Ultra-soft short plush / crystal plush, ~280-330 gsm, skin-friendly, no shedding or odor

☁️
填充Filling

优质 PP 棉，蓬松无异味回弹好。禁止回收棉/黑心棉。站姿款可少量环保塑料粒增加稳定性（需内胆双层缝制）Premium PP cotton, fluffy with good rebound. No recycled or substandard cotton. Small eco pellets allowed for standing stability (double-layered inner bag required)

🪡
刺绣与配件Embroidery & Accessories

眼睛鼻子嘴巴：电脑刺绣。眼镜/小花/蝴蝶结：软布贴绣或绒布填充。所有小配件软质固定，儿童款需通过拉力测试Features: machine embroidery. Accessories: soft applique or plush-filled. All small parts fixed soft; pull-test required for children's products
