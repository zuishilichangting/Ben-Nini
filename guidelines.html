🚫 角色禁区Character Red Lines

以下规则适用于所有授权合作、衍生品设计和内容创作。违反将导致授权终止。These rules apply to all licensing, derivative designs, and content creation. Violations will result in termination.

✅ 允许Allowed
  • 可改变服装、场景、职业（如宇航员Ben、厨师Ben、圣诞Ben）Change outfits, scenes, occupations (Astronaut Ben, Chef Ben, Christmas Ben)
  • 可做温馨、幽默、成长、治愈内容Warm, humorous, growth, healing content
  • 可开发季节限定、节日限定、城市限定款Seasonal, holiday, city-exclusive editions
  • 可添加角色，前提是新角色代表一种新的情感价值Add characters if they represent a new emotional value
❌ 禁止Prohibited
  • ❌ 改变角色核心人格（Ben=可靠 / Nini=温柔 / Caibao=快乐）❌ Change core personality (Ben=reliable / Nini=gentle / Caibao=joyful)
  • ❌ Ben 做霸总/高冷/凶狠/腹黑❌ Ben as domineering/cold/aggressive/scheming
  • ❌ Nini 做女王/刻薄/恶毒❌ Nini as queen/mean/malicious
  • ❌ Caibao 做心机/负能量/阴阳怪气❌ Caibao as manipulative/negative/sarcastic
  • ❌ 加入魔法/超能力/反派设定❌ Add magic/superpowers/villains
  • ❌ 狗血剧情、霸凌、价值观攻击❌ Melodrama, bullying, value attacks
